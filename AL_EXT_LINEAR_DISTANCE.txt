9.3. AL_EXT_LINEAR_DISTANCE
An OpenAL 1.1 implementation will always support the AL_EXT_LINEAR_DISTANCE extension. This allows an application written to the OpenAL 1.0 specification to access the offset abilities expressed in sections 3.4.3 and 3.4.4.

Tokens:
AL_LINEAR_DIST ANCE AL_LINEAR_DISTANCE_CLAMPED
